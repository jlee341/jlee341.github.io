Good Site:Apple

Apple.com is the official online home of Apple inc, offering a sleek showcase of their latest products, services and support.

  • C:Contrast: Each element are very different from eachother and easy to read
  • R:Repetition: All the buttons are square and the font colors are repeated, making he site look neat and uniform
  • A:Alignment: Each element is aligned accordingly to eachother and connects well to the other pages
  • P:Proximity: The similar elements are close in proximity to eachother while the different elements are far apart enough to tell they are different

Bad Site:CNN

CNN.com is the digital hub for CNN providing up-to-date news of events occurring all around the world of all topics including world events, politics, and business.

  • C:Contrast: The elements all merge into one blob of text with images scattered making it hard to navigate
  • R:Repetition: There are images with headlines below them and that goes on for the whole pages but the image sizes vary, and not all headlines below each image relate to the image.
  • A:Alignment: Each row is aligned differently than the last, doesn't look very neat or uniform at all.
  • P:Proximity: Each element is very close to eachother making it hard to tell what topic is related and what isn't.
