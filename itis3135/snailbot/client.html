About Snailbot

Meet SnailBot! -- The best Discord bot for displaying the latest news from War Thunder, Crossout, Steam games and more. SnailBot will keep your server updated with the latest news from your favorite games, and setup is super, super easy! With the use of a single command, SnailBot will guide you through the process of setting up a feed for your server. You can even setup multiple ones if you want, and in different languages as well!
